Controls:

"Space" or Virtual Button "shot": Fire projectile
"W & D" or "Up & Down": Forwards and backwards movement
"A & S" or "Left & Right": Rotate
"Esc" ingame to open settings menu

Details:

-Three lifes, everytime you touch an enemy you lost a heart, until you lost three of them and die
-Asteroids give 1 score points when destroyed with shots.
-UFO follows you and give 10 score points.
-When you reach 6 or more score points you shot two projectiles everytime you fire, 11 or more you shot three.
-The Highscore is persistent, even when you close the application it stores the data and gives your best high score.
